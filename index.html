About Us

We are a 6-member artist management non-profit in the Delhi-NCR space. We bridge the gap between upcoming artists and venues, and seek to improve the live music culture and independent music culture through curated events.

For Artists

We find the best ways and places for you to get your music out there, all at your convenience.

For Venues

We bring you the freshest pick of independent musicians from across the region.

For Everyone

We aim to build and nurture a thriving community of independent music enthusiasts.

Team


A musician, maverick, aesthetic genius, number magician, financial analyst and engineer came together in July 2019 with one common thread: their passion for music, especially when done live. Currently based in Ashoka University, we each wear multiple hats in the conception and development of what we'd like to see in Delhi's live music scene.

AUDITION FOR US

If you're interested in opportunities to perform at some of the best venues in the Delhi-NCR region, send us a video! Here are the rules:



  • Original music preferred
  • Send all entries to the30thJulyProject@gmail.com
  • Number: 2 videos (total) per person
  • Format: MPEG4, AVI, 3GP and YouTube links
  • Size: 100MB (total)
  • No karaoke tracks allowed
  • Artist details (to be written in the message body):
    • Name
    • Contact number
    • Email address
  • Setup: Our events are held in an intimate setting, so acoustic instruments and a minimalistic set up are encouraged.
